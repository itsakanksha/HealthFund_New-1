ABOUT

HealthFund aims to promote Scientists, Patients, and Investors collaboration to collectively expedite the path of medicinal innovations into the clinic. HealthFund allows investors to contact researchers online and sign the NDA (non-disclosure agreement) remotely. To potentially present research discoveries in an easy-to-view and understand manner. Additionally, to allow philanthropic investments at different stages of health-tech development.
